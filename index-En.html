General information

This Internet resource was created in order to obtain information about the sights of the city of St. Petersburg.

St. Petersburg – one of the most beautiful megalopolises in the world, to see which travelers come from all over the world. In 2015 alone, over 3.7 million Russian and 2.8 million foreign tourists visited it. Spread out on the coast of the Gulf of Finland, at the mouth of the Neva River, St. Petersburg is the second largest city in Russia (in the status of an independent subject of the federation) and at the same time the administrative center of the Leningrad Region and the Northwestern Federal District.
